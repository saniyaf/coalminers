India's leading producer of Iron ore

Sesa Goa Iron Ore also has two power plants of 30 MW each that produce 60 MW of clean power by using the waste heat recovery from its coke ovens and blast furnace gas. The company has diversified its product portfolio with the addition of cement, nickel-cobalt operations.

  • Iron ore is found in nearly every industrial activity
  • India's iron ore reserves rank 7th globally (i.e. 5.5 bn tonnes)
  • It has a wide range of applications in steelmaking, construction, infrastructure, and the automotive sector

Fuelling India’s energy needs

With a vision to unlock India’s latent energy , Cairn Oil & Gas is the largest private sector producer of crude oil in India. In a market where over 85% of oil and 54% of gas demands are met with imported resources,Miners Ltd., plays an active role in reducing our country’s import burden by producing oil and gas locally.

  • Largest private oil and gas exploration and production company in India
  • India’s petroleum products consumption during FY'2021 was ~194 million MT
  • Cairn contributed to approximately 25% of India’s crude oil production in FY'2020

Enabling a self-sustained nation

Our business unit consists of Copper Smelter, Refinery, Sulphuric acid plant, Phosphoric acid plant, and Copper Rod plant at Tuticorin in southern India and a Refinery and two Copper Rod plants at Silvassa in western India.

  • Copper is an essential commodity of everyday life
  • Copper is a wonder metal with anti-corrosive properties
  • Copper is also used for making cables, transformers, castings, motors and castings, and alloy-based products

Enriching lives with power

India is the third-largest power producer in the world. However, the demand for power in India far outweighs the supply which leads to substantial power outages. Over 280 million people in India have no access to electricity. With the Government of India advancing its plans to reduce the power shortage, Miners Power steps in & caters to the demand by establishing power generation plants across the country
